      FAQ

      Questions we get a lot

      Is this tool free to use?

      Yes — 100% free, no account needed, no hidden charges. Paste and check, that's it.

      Does it store the text I paste?

      No. The text you submit is only processed in memory to generate a result and is never saved.

      Can it guarantee a post is safe?

      No tool can guarantee that. FAKECUT flags known warning patterns — always do your own verification by checking the company's official website and LinkedIn.

      What if my post scores low but still feels off?

      Trust your instincts. A low score means fewer red-flag patterns were detected — not that the opportunity is verified.

      Does it work for scholarships and freelance gigs too?

      Yes! The rules cover job posts, internships, scholarship announcements, and freelance offers. Scam tactics repeat across all these categories.

      What does the risk score mean?

      0–30 is "Likely Safe", 31–60 is "Caution", and 61–100 is "Suspicious". Higher scores mean more scam signals were found.

      Can I check just a URL without the full text?

      Yes. Paste the URL in the URL field and leave the text box empty. The tool will analyse the domain for shortened links and suspicious patterns.
